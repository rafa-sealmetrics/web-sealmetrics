How we calculate

The math behind the numbers.

When you enter your traffic and revenue, we apply three conservative estimates based on aggregate data from our client base:

01

Hidden traffic estimate

EU eCommerce average consent rate: 45-55%. We apply a conservative uplift to your reported GA4 sessions based on your market's specific consent block rate.

Conservative estimate Market-specific rates Based on client data
02

Implied conversion rate

Your revenue divided by visible sessions equals your current conversion rate. We apply this same rate to the hidden traffic to estimate the revenue you're not seeing.
