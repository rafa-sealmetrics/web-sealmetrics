The Blind Spot

Most teams don't realize how much data they're losing.

In 2018, GDPR changed the rules. Cookie consent became mandatory. Overnight, analytics platforms started losing visitors.

Today, 60-87% of EU visitors reject cookies. For those visitors, your analytics record nothing — no pageviews, no conversions, no revenue attribution. They become invisible.

You're making million-euro decisions based on data that represents, at best, 40% of your actual traffic. Your ROAS calculations are wrong. Your attribution is broken. Your forecasts are built on incomplete data.

"The French data protection authority (CNIL) has explicitly recommended that organizations consider alternatives to Google Analytics that do not require consent mechanisms."

— CNIL Guidelines on Cookies and Trackers, 2024

SealMetrics was built to solve this. Zero cookies. Zero consent requirements. 100% of your traffic captured. Not by circumventing privacy laws, but by designing a system that doesn't need personal data to give you accurate analytics.
